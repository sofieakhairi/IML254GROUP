Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase Nebula E-Gift Cards?

You can purchase Nebula E-Gift Cards online via our websites. At the moment, the purchase of Nebula E-Gift Cards is only available at Nebula's Malaysia. Nebula will only honour Nebula E-Gift Cards purchased at our stores and website. You cannot use a Nebula E-Gift Card to purchase Sephora E-Gift Cards.

Are there any fees associated with the Nebula E-Gift Card?

No. You will only be charged the amount mentioned on the Nebula E-Gift Card.

 Can I choose the value of a Nebula E-Gift Card I want to purchase?

Yes. You can purchase a Sephora E-Gift Card of any amount above the minimum purchase amount of RM20 and the maximum amount of RM500.

What payment methods can be used?

We only accept VISA and MasterCard.

Can I buy a Nebula E-Gift Card for myself?

Yes, you can. Simply enter your email address in the recipient field. Upon completion of the purchase, the Nebula E-Gift Card will be delivered to your email.
